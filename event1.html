CHEM-E-DEXTERITY
Ever thought of making a mini-pump of your own? If yes, Noesis’19 brings to you a thrilling opportunity to make your own water pump! It’s a high time to design and construct your water pump and also to learn about the industrial applications and principles of various equipment . Along with that you will also be given a brief theoretical knowledge about the variables involved in the process and also about the transport phenomena by which will be helpful to understand how the mobility of extensive thermodynamic property cause changes.

OBJECTIVE: Workshop on working principle and industrial applications of pumps and other transport equipment such as valves.

AIM: To make participants learn about the industrial applications of the equipments involved in the transport phenomena To make them aware of the underlying principles of various equipments To make them familiar with the design aspects variables involved in the process To illustrate them with real small scale working model and help them designing and understanding the same.
